Our Listings

By Johnny on April 14, 2015

A passion for automotive beauty, power, aesthetics, and prestige.

Each hypercar brings the owner into the stratosphere of a multitude of arenas. Highly limited production numbers ensure exclusivity and investment quality of your purchase. For example, Ferrari produces 9000 examples a year while Pagani has made just 100.

Unmatched performance pushing the pinnacle of engineering.

Below are 3 manufacturers currently at the pinnacle of hypercars performance.

Bugatti

First started in Germany in 1909, now one of the few marques whose sale price for a new car exceeds $2.5M. Resale value of the car is has exceeded $3.3M currently.

Koenigsegg

Pushing the limits of Swedish engineering to create a hypercar, Koenigsegg is no stranger to performance. Optional driver training with a factory driver to ensure an understanding of the power and handling is learned.

Pagani

The latest offering is for the Huayra, first car with independently movable aero spoilers on the front and rear of the car to provide maximum downforce. Initally sold for $1.3M, the price on current markets is around $2.2M

There are many more brand which can cater to and fulfill your desires beyond these three. Let us assist you in helping identify your desire and select your choice. Here are 3 more Makes for your consideration.
